New York City

The size of New York is 468sq miles with land size of 302sq. miles and water size of 165sq miles. The largest borough in New York City is 109 square miles by area. By Population, Brooklyn is considered the largest borough with a population over 2.6million people. New York City uses Eastern Time for their timezone. New York is known for its tourism. There many tourist areas, such as Times Square, and Brooklyn Bridge.
